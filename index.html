About Awantrix

Awantrix was founded by security practitioners and former buyers of penetration testing services who were frustrated by opaque pricing, generic scopes, and long, sales-driven processes. After running dozens of pen tests across startups and enterprises, our team saw the same pattern repeat: security leaders spending weeks navigating vendor pitches just to get to a clear view of options. We built Awantrix to transform that experience into a structured, transparent marketplace that respects your time and risk.

Our mission is to help every organization—from early-stage companies to global enterprises—run penetration testing that is aligned with real-world threats and business objectives. We believe in buyer-first design, rigorous vetting of providers, and uncompromising security standards. At our core, we value clarity, integrity, and measurable impact. Everything we build is aimed at giving security teams more signal, less noise, and a faster path to meaningful security improvements.
