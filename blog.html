Getting Started with Our Platform

This guide demystifies internal vs external penetration testing so security leaders can invest where it matters most. It explains how external tests probe internet-facing assets, while internal tests reveal lateral movement, privilege escalation, and data exfiltration risks after a breach. Using patterns from hundreds of Awantrix engagements, it outlines when to prioritize each, how different industries blend them, and what “good” scopes and reports look like. Finally, it shows how the Awantrix pen test marketplace standardizes scopes, pricing, and provider strengths, helping teams design the right mix of tests faster and with greater confidence.
